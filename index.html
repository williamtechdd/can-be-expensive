Simple Steps to Restore Your Childhood Memories

Restoring old family photos with Remini is straightforward if you follow a few basic steps. First, collect your printed pictures and gently clean off any dust with a soft cloth so marks don't show up in the scan. Use your phone's camera or a scanner app to capture a clear photo of each print, making sure there is good lighting and minimal glare on the surface. Try to keep the camera steady and hold it parallel to the picture for best results.

Next, open the Remini app and upload the scanned image. Choose the enhancement or restoration option and let the AI analyze the photo. Within a few seconds, you'll see a before‑and‑after preview highlighting sharper faces, improved textures, and more balanced colors. At this stage, simply save the improved version to your gallery or a dedicated folder for restored family memories. Many people like to organize these by year, event, or family member to keep everything easy to find.

Tips for Better Restored Results

To get the best possible outcome, start with the clearest scan you can manage. Take photos of your prints in natural light near a window, avoid strong reflections, and crop out any background that isn't part of the original picture. This gives the AI more useful information to work with. If a photo is badly torn, you can gently align the pieces before scanning so the app understands the full scene.
